Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?
Delivery times vary depending on your location and restaurant preparation time. Typically, deliveries arrive within 30-45 minutes.
What areas do you deliver to?
We currently deliver within a 10-mile radius of our partner restaurants. You can check if your address is within our delivery area during checkout.
How can I track my order?
Once your order is confirmed, you'll receive a tracking link via SMS or email. You can also track your order in real-time through your profile page.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay. Cash on delivery is also available for select restaurants.
Can I modify or cancel my order?
You can modify or cancel your order within 5 minutes of placing it. After that, please contact our customer support team for assistance.
